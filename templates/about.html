Project Vision

Smartech Analytics was born from the recognition that marketing leaders need powerful, intuitive tools to make sense of their campaign data. By combining the analytical power of Gemini AI with the robust data storage of AlloyDB, this platform enables marketing professionals to ask questions in plain English and receive instant, actionable insights.

This proof of concept demonstrates how AI can transform traditional marketing analytics from static reports to dynamic, conversational intelligence. Marketing teams can now explore their data naturally, uncover hidden patterns, and make data-driven decisions faster than ever before.
